### Mining War Objectives

The Mining War centers on the control of **Excavators**. Unlike standard mobs, these structures lose health based on the **number of hits** rather than raw damage numbers. This mechanic ensures that large groups of players, regardless of individual power levels, can contribute to the faction's victory.

- **Airfield Access**: Opens 10 minutes after the war begins. Use flight to flank enemy bases.
- **Barricades & Turrets**: Defeating the Holy Stone Keeper activates your faction's turrets, which automatically destroy enemy barricades.
- **Mercenary System**: Guild leaders can recruit solo players to bolster their ranks, offering rewards ranging from 100 to 100,000 Diamonds.
