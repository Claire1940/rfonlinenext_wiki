<Section icon={Shield} title="Biosuits: Roles, Swapping & Strategic Use">
Biosuits are the core of RF Online Next's class system, offering flexible role-switching and specialized combat styles. Understanding how to utilize different Biosuits is vital for both PvE and large-scale PvP engagements. There are seven distinct Biosuit types, each with unique capabilities.

<Callout type="tip">
Efficient Biosuit swapping is key to success. Don't be afraid to switch roles during combat to adapt to changing situations, whether it's for bursting down a boss, providing support, or engaging in aerial dogfights.
</Callout>

### Biosuit Overview

| Biosuit | Role | Combat Range | Beginner Friendliness | PvE Use | PvP Use |
|:--------|:-----|:-------------|:----------------------|:--------|:--------|
| **Demolisher** | Mid-to-long-range physical AoE | Mid to Long | Very High | Mob clearing, field farming | Area pressure, backline fire support |
| **Punisher** | Stable ranged damage & ally cover | Ranged | High | Safe questing, consistent attacks | Cover fire, crowd control |
| **Enforcer** | Frontline guardian & tank | Close | High (for durable play) | Survival, multi-enemy pulls | Shielding, taunt pressure |
| **Dreadnought** | Charging melee destroyer | Close | Medium-High | Melee questing, direct burst | Chasing targets, frontline damage |
| **Psypher** | Force-based battlefield control | Mid | Medium | Grouped enemy control, tactical clearing | Formation disruption, crowd control |
| **Technician** | Support, healing, drones | Ranged Support | Medium (for party play) | Party support, healing, utility | Sustain support, defensive utility |
| **Phantom** | High-speed assassin | Close | Advanced | Fast single-target eliminations | Backline picks, high-risk engages |
| **Arbiter** | Close-to-mid-range hybrid | Close to Mid | Medium | Flexible quest combat, stylish play | Pressure tools, flexible skirmishing |

### Strategic Biosuit Swapping

| Scenario | Recommended Biosuit Strategy |
|:---------|:-----------------------------|
| **Solo Questing/Farming** | Use **Demolisher** or **Punisher** for efficient, safe ranged damage. |
| **Dungeon Bosses** | Swap to **Enforcer** for tanking or **Dreadnought** for burst melee. |
| **Large-Scale PvP** | Combine **Enforcer** (frontline), **Demolisher** (AoE), and **Technician** (support) for balanced team. |
| **Aerial Combat** | All Biosuits support flight, but practice with your preferred damage dealer to master airborne maneuvers. |
| **Support Role** | **Technician** is crucial for healing and buffs in group content. |
