<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Your Biosuit Progression">
Maximizing your Biosuit's potential involves more than just selecting a high-tier option. It requires strategic progression in gear, skills, and understanding the Biosuit swapping mechanic.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose a Main Damage Biosuit First">
    Focus your early resources on one primary damage-dealing Biosuit, such as **Demolisher** or **Punisher**. This provides a stable foundation for questing, farming, and contributing to early boss encounters. Avoid spreading your upgrade materials too thin across multiple Biosuits initially.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Biosuit Swapping for Tactical Roles">
    Biosuit swapping is not just a gimmick; it's a core combat tool. Keep a defensive or support Biosuit ready for specific situations like PvP, party play, or bosses with challenging mechanics. For example, switch to **Enforcer** for tanking or **Technician** for healing when needed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Gear Enhancement">
    When progression slows, focus on enhancing your currently equipped gear. Equipment Enhancement, including Prime Mods and Random Options, often provides a more immediate power boost than chasing entirely new setups. Use the Equipment Enhancement menu to keep your active loadout competitive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Invest in Active Skills">
    Power up the skills you frequently use with your main Biosuit. This includes Adding Skills, Skill Enhancements, and Add-Ons. Tailor Add-ons to your current objective, whether it's maximizing damage for farming or enhancing control for PvP. Avoid upgrading unused skills simply because they have a notification.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Boost Collection Stats: Attack & Accuracy">
    Collection progress provides passive stats. Prioritize attack and accuracy first for consistent damage output. Attack directly increases your damage, while accuracy ensures your hits land effectively, making all other damage upgrades more impactful. After these, consider durability or evasion based on your specific needs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Integrate Rovers for Passive Growth">
    Rovers are companion units that offer valuable stat bonuses, such as EXP gain, healing item capacity, and attack boosts. Align your Rover growth with your main Biosuit build to gain passive advantages rather than treating them as mere cosmetic additions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Smart Resource Management">
Effective progression in RF Online Next is about smart resource allocation. Focus on strengthening your core combat role and unlocking essential utility swaps before diversifying your Biosuit upgrades. This approach prevents common bottlenecks and ensures smoother progress.
</Callout>

</Section>
