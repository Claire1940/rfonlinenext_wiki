<Section icon={Swords} title="Technician's Performance in PvE and PvP">
The Technician's performance varies significantly between solo PvE and group PvP/PvE content. While they can solo quest, their true potential shines when paired with other Biosuits.

### PvE Utility
In PvE, the Technician provides invaluable support for dungeon runs, world boss raids, and challenging quests. Their healing capabilities reduce downtime and their drones can add consistent damage or draw aggro, making fights smoother for the entire party.

| PvE Use Case | Technician Role | Key Benefits |
|---|---|---|
| **Dungeon Runs** | Primary Healer/Support | Sustains party, reduces potion consumption, enables harder content. |
| **World Bosses** | Buffs, Healing, Minor DPS | Keeps damage dealers alive, provides utility for long fights. |
| **Field Farming** | Limited Solo Potential | Slower clear speed compared to DPS Biosuits, but can sustain well. |
| **Questing (Group)** | Essential Support | Ensures smooth progression, especially in challenging areas. |

### PvP Impact
In large-scale PvP battles, the Technician can be a game-changer. Their ability to heal multiple allies, deploy defensive structures, and disrupt enemy formations with drones makes them a high-value target and a critical component of any successful warband.

| PvP Use Case | Technician Role | Key Benefits |
|---|---|---|
| **Mining War** | Defensive Anchor | Protects excavators, heals frontline, deploys defensive turrets. |
| **Faction Battles** | Group Healer/Buffer | Keeps allies in the fight, turns the tide with timely support. |
| **Aerial Combat** | Ranged Support | Can heal and buff flying allies, deploy drones for air control. |
| **Skirmishes** | Sustain & Zone | Provides survivability, can create safe zones with towers. |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Building Your RF Online Next Technician">
Optimizing your Technician involves careful consideration of skill allocation, gear, and Biosuit swapping strategies. Focus on enhancing your support capabilities while ensuring you have enough survivability to stay in the fight.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Core Support Skills">
    Invest skill points into your primary healing abilities, drone deployment skills, and tower construction. These are the bread and butter of the Technician's support kit. Look for skills that increase healing output, drone duration, or tower effectiveness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enhance Survivability Gear">
    As a support, you'll often be targeted. Prioritize gear enhancements that boost your defense, HP, and damage resistance. This allows you to withstand incoming damage and continue supporting your team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimize Collection Stats">
    Focus on collection stats that directly benefit your role. While attack and accuracy are important for damage dealers, Technicians should prioritize stats that improve healing, skill cooldown reduction, and defensive attributes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Rovers for Utility">
    Rovers can provide additional utility. Look for Rovers that offer EXP gain for faster leveling, healing item capacity, or defensive bonuses. Align Rover growth with your support build to maximize passive benefits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Strategic Biosuit Swapping">
    While Technician is your main support role, having a damage-focused Biosuit as a secondary option can help with solo content or when your party needs a temporary damage boost. Practice swapping efficiently.
  </Step>
</Steps>
