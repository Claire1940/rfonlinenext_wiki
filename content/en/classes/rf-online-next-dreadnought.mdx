<Section icon={Trophy} title="Dreadnought PvP & Mining War Strategy">

The true testing ground for the **rf online next dreadnought** is the massive, faction-based PvP battlegrounds and the highly competitive Mining Wars (Faction Wars). These battlegrounds pit thousands of players from the Bellato, Cora, and Accretia factions against each other in a brutal struggle for resources and honor.

In Faction Wars, the primary objective centers around excavators. Unlike standard targets, excavators lose health based on the number of hits they receive rather than the raw damage value of those hits. Additionally, the Holy Stone Keeper spawns in the center of the map one minute after the war begins, offering massive combat buffs to the faction that lands the final hit. As a Dreadnought, your job is to lead the charge, disrupt enemy defenders, and secure these vital objectives.
