The strength of your **rf online next biosuit** is determined by several layers of growth. Because suits can be swapped, you should focus on "Account-Wide" progression systems that benefit every role you play. This includes gear enhancement, skill growth, and the Rover companion system.

**Priority Growth Layers:**
1. **Gear Enhancement**: Prioritize your active weapon and chest piece. Use "Batch Enhance" for efficiency but watch your material costs.
2. **Skill Power**: Focus on the active skills of your S-Tier suits first. Add-ons should be tuned for damage uptime in PvE.
3. **Collection Stats**: This is the secret to high-end power. Completing collections for **Attack** and **Accuracy** ensures you never miss a hit against higher-level bosses.
4. **Rovers**: These floating companions provide passive EXP gain and attack stats. Treat them as essential stat-sticks, not just decorations.

A Biosuit alone is often not enough to turn the tide of a massive Faction War. Players must master **Sacred Weapons**—large-scale battlefield tools that provide immense firepower or defensive utility. These systems are unlocked through specific quest milestones like "Bellato's Ammunition Depot."

**The Three Pillars of Sacred Warfare:**
- **MAU (Mechanic Armor Unit)**: These are piloted machines. They are best for charging enemy lines and forcing defensive formations to break.
- **Launcher**: A heavy fire-support weapon. It excels at cutting off retreat routes and obliterating grouped enemies from a distance.
- **Animus**: A summoned power that seizes control of the battlefield. Use it during pivotal moments in a fight to force enemy repositioning.
