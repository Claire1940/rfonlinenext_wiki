### The Three Pillars of Sacred Power

**1. MAU (Mechanic Armor Unit)**
The MAU is a piloted giant mech designed for frontline assault. It is best used by players in the Enforcer or Dreadnought suits who need to break through enemy barricades. It offers massive durability and direct control.

**2. Launcher**
The Launcher provides heavy fire support. It is a stationary or slow-moving artillery unit that obliterates defenses from a distance. It pairs perfectly with the Demolisher or Punisher for maximum area denial.

**3. Animus**
Animus is a summoned entity that provides immense burst presence and battlefield control. It is often used to seize objectives during the **Mining War** when multiple factions are clashing over excavators.
