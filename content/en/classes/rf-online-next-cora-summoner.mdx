In *RF Online Next*, **Sacred Weapons** are not standard gear slots. They are battlefield-scale tools designed for precise timing, positioning, and objective pressure. The **Animus** stands alongside the piloted **MAU** (Mechanic Armor Unit) and the heavy fire-support **Launcher** as one of the three primary Sacred Weapons. 

When deployed, the Animus functions as an immense presence that can completely turn the tide of a stalled group fight. It forces enemy formations to scatter, inflicts massive burst damage, and establishes absolute zone control. Understanding how to unlock, charge, and deploy this mechanical god is crucial for any competitive player.

| Sacred Weapon | Battlefield Role | Primary Strategic Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **MAU** | Frontline assault and direct piloted control | Breaking defensive lines and direct frontline engagement |
| **Launcher** | Heavy ranged fire-support | Lane denial and covering objective retreat paths |
| **Animus** | Large-scale burst and battlefield control | Turning major team fights and forcing enemy repositioning |
