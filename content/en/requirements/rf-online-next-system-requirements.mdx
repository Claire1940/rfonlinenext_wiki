<Section icon={Shield} title="Technical Reliability and Security">

Security and stability are paramount in a competitive MMORPG where faction honor and Mining War rewards are at stake. Netmarble employs several layers of protection to ensure a fair playing field, which may occasionally interact with your system software.

- **Anti-Cheat Compatibility**: Ensure your firewall allows the Netmarble Launcher and the game executable. Some aggressive antivirus software may flag the anti-cheat module as a false positive.
- **Network Stability**: A wired Ethernet connection is highly recommended for PC. The game uses a high tick-rate for its "hit-count" based excavator combat in Mining Wars; packet loss can result in "ghost" hits that don't register.
- **Remote Play Security**: When using Netmarble Connect to play remotely, ensure your PC is not in sleep mode and that your mobile device is using a secure connection.

| Technical Issue | Common Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Game Won't Launch** | Update Graphics Drivers to the latest June 2026 version. |
| **Frequent Crashes** | Verify game files through the Epic Games Store or Netmarble Launcher. |
| **High Latency** | Check if you are on the correct regional server (NA/EU/ASIA). |
| **Low FPS on High End** | Disable 'V-Sync' and check for background CPU-heavy applications. |
