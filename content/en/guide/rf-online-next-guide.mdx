<Section icon={Map} title="PvP Warfare and Mining War Strategy">

The endgame of **rf online next** centers on the Faction War (Mining War). This is a massive conflict where the Accretia, Bellato, and Cora factions fight for control over excavators and resources. Victory requires more than just high stats; it requires tactical coordination and understanding of the Holy Stone Keeper mechanics.

<Callout type="warning" title="Flight Fuel Management">
While the airfield connects faction bases, flying too high or for too long will deplete your fuel. Fuel shortage at high altitudes results in instant death, so always plan your flight path carefully.
</Callout>

| War Mechanic | Timing/Rule | Strategic Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Airfield Opening** | 10 mins after start | Enables flanking and vertical attacks |
| **Holy Stone Keeper** | 1 min after start | Grants massive combat buffs to the final-hit faction |
| **Excavators** | Win Condition | HP is reduced by hit count, not raw damage |
| **Mercenary System** | Cutoff 21:10 | Guilds can hire solo players for Diamond rewards |

**Mining War Tips:**
- **Focus Fire**: Since excavators lose HP based on the number of hits, high-attack-speed Biosuits are essential for destroying objectives quickly.
- **Turret Control**: Defeating the Holy Stone Keeper activates your faction's turrets, which automatically destroy enemy barricades.
- **Role Swapping**: If your team is pushing, swap to an offensive Biosuit. If you are defending an excavator, swap to a support or tank role to maximize survival time.
