  <Step num="1" title="The Level 5 Threshold">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Biosuit Flight">
    Finish the "Colleague in Danger" quest. This is the flight tutorial, essential for navigating dungeons with verticality and avoiding ground-based boss AOE attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Biosuit Equipping">
    Complete the "Bellato's Ammunition Depot" quest. This tutorial teaches you how to swap between your 7 available Biosuits, a mechanic that is vital for surviving different dungeon boss phases.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ancient Ruins Boss Check">
    Defeat the Holy Stone Keeper in the Arcane Ruins quest. This is your first true "boss dungeon" experience, testing your ability to manage movement, flight, and damage uptime.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Sacred Weapon Deployment">
    Perform your first five Sacred Weapon deployments (MAU, Launcher, or Animus). This milestone is required to handle the high HP pools found in mid-to-late game World Bosses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Progression Lock">

</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="World Boss Contribution and Reward Rules">

World Bosses represent the pinnacle of group PvE in RF Online Next. Unlike traditional dungeons, World Bosses occur in open or semi-open zones where contribution is the primary metric for rewards. To maximize your gains, you must understand the rules of engagement and the tiered reward system.

| Contribution Type | Participation Requirement | Reward Tier | Distribution Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Individual** | Land hits and stay in zone | Tier 3 (Basic) | Direct Mailbox |
| **Party** | Combined damage/healing score | Tier 2 (Advanced) | Party Loot / Mail |
| **Raid Group** | Top-tier damage contribution | Tier 1 (Elite) | Guild Auction / Direct |

The contribution system tracks your activity throughout the fight. If you leave the map or disconnect during the encounter, your contribution may reset, potentially disqualifying you from the highest reward tiers.
