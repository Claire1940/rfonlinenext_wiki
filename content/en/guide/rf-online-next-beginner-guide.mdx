<Section icon={Shield} title="Understanding Biosuits and Sacred Weapons">
RF Online Next's unique class system revolves around Biosuits, which allow on-the-fly role switching. Alongside these, Sacred Weapons provide battlefield-altering power, crucial for large-scale engagements.

### Biosuit Roles and Beginner Friendliness

Biosuits are your primary means of combat and role-playing. Each offers a distinct playstyle. Here's a quick overview of how they stack up for new players.

| Biosuit     | Role                               | Range          | Beginner Fit | PvE Use                                 | PvP Use                                    |
| :---------- | :--------------------------------- | :------------- | :----------- | :-------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------- |
| **Demolisher**  | Mid-to-long-range physical AoE damage | Mid to long    | Very High    | Mob clearing, field farming, dungeon waves | Area pressure, clustered-target damage     |
| **Punisher**    | Stable ranged damage, ally cover   | Ranged         | High         | Safe questing, consistent attacks       | Cover fire, crowd control pressure         |
| **Enforcer**    | Frontline guardian and tank        | Close          | High         | Survival, multi-enemy pulls             | Shielding, taunt pressure, disruption      |
| **Dreadnought** | Charging melee destroyer           | Close          | Medium-High  | Melee questing, direct burst            | Chasing targets, combo pressure            |
| **Psypher**     | Force-based battlefield control    | Mid            | Medium       | Grouped enemy control, tactical clearing | Formation disruption, crowd control        |
| **Technician**  | Support, healing, drones           | Ranged support | Medium       | Party support, healing, utility         | Sustain support, defensive utility         |
| **Arbiter**     | Close-to-mid-range hybrid combat   | Close to mid   | Medium       | Hybrid damage routes, flexible combat   | Pressure tools, target control             |
| **Phantom**     | High-speed assassin                | Close          | Advanced     | Fast single-target eliminations         | Backline picks, burst windows, high-risk   |

<Callout type="info" title="Biosuit Swapping Strategy">
You can swap Biosuits from the Biosuit menu to adapt to different combat situations. While it's tempting to upgrade all of them, focus on one or two primary damage-dealing Biosuits first for efficient early progression.
</Callout>

Effective progression in RF Online Next involves smart resource allocation and understanding how different systems contribute to your character's power. It's about optimizing your Biosuits, skills, gear, and Rovers.

### Smart Progression Choices

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Prioritize Main Biosuit">
    Invest in the Biosuit you use most for quests and dungeons. Firepower-oriented suits like **Demolisher** or **Punisher** are excellent for solo progress.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Strategic Biosuit Swapping">
    Use Biosuit swapping for tactical advantage in different situations (damage for farming, defense for PvP). Avoid spreading upgrade materials too thin across all Biosuits early on.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gear Enhancement First">
    When progress slows, focus on enhancing your equipped gear. Safe enhancement of active equipment is often more efficient than seeking new, un-upgraded items.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Skill Power-Up">
    Prioritize upgrading the active skills of your main Biosuit. Skill Add-ons should support your role, whether it's damage uptime, crowd control, or survival.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="tip" title="Collection Stats">
Focus on increasing **Attack** and **Accuracy** first in your collection stats for consistent damage. After these, branch into durability or evasion based on your playstyle needs.
</Callout>

### Rover Utility

Rovers are companion units that provide practical stat and utility bonuses. Don't treat them as mere cosmetics.

| Rover System   | Function                                                                           | Benefits                                                                                                 |
| :------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Rover**      | Floating companion unit                                                            | Provides passive stat boosts, such as EXP gain and healing item capacity.                                |
| **Fuse**       | Combines Rovers for upgrades                                                       | Enhances Rover stats and unlocks higher-tier bonuses.                                                    |
| **Collection** | Completing Rover sets                                                              | Grants additional passive stats to your character.                                                       |
| **Finalize**   | Locks in Rover stats                                                               | Optimizes a Rover's potential, making its bonuses permanent and more potent.                             |
| **Tuning**     | Adjusts Rover attributes                                                           | Allows customization of Rover stats to better suit your main Biosuit and playstyle.                      |
| **Workshop**   | Crafting and upgrading Rover components                                            | Creates and improves parts for your Rovers, further enhancing their capabilities.                        |
| **Rover Burst**| Temporary powerful boost                                                           | Activates a short-term surge of power or utility from your Rover during critical combat situations.      |
