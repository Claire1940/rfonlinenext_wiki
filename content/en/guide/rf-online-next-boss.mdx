### World Boss Contribution Rules

Successfully defeating a World Boss isn't just about dealing damage; it's about **contribution**. Rewards are distributed based on your participation, whether you're fighting solo, in a party, or as part of a larger raid group.

| Contribution Type | Reward Distribution | Key Action | Notes |
| :---------------- | :------------------ | :--------- | :---- |
| **Individual**    | Personal rewards    | Deal damage, survive | Best for solo players, focus on consistent output. |
| **Party**         | Shared party rewards | Coordinate attacks, support allies | Essential for tackling harder bosses, ensures more reliable rewards. |
| **Raid Group**    | Guild auction/shared rewards | Massive coordinated effort | Highest potential rewards, often involves guild-wide strategies. |

Staying near the boss and actively engaging is paramount. Changing maps during a fight will often invalidate your contribution, leading to missed rewards.
