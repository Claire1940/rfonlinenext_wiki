Victory in the chip war is determined by the survival of your faction's excavators. If all opposing excavators are destroyed, your faction wins instantly. If time expires, the faction with the highest remaining excavator HP percentage is declared the victor.

| Feature | Description |
| :--- | :--- |

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategic Tip">

<Section icon={Zap} title="The Holy Stone Keeper and Battlefield Buffs">

The Holy Stone Keeper is the central boss of the **rf online next chip war**. It spawns in the middle of the map exactly one minute after the war begins. This entity is not just a target for loot; it is a tactical tool that provides the "final hit" faction with overwhelming power.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement">
    Move to the center map immediately upon war start. The first spawn occurs at the 1-minute mark.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Final Hit Push">
    Coordinate your faction's burst damage. Only the faction that lands the final hit receives the Holy Stone Keeper's blessing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Buff Accumulation">
    The buff scales with each successful kill. It improves Movement Speed, Attack, Defense, Accuracy, and Evasion across multiple tiers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Turret Activation">
    Securing the kill activates your faction's turrets, which automatically target enemy barricades to open new attack paths.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Buff Tier | Stat Increases | Secondary Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | +5% Attack / Defense | Minor Speed boost |
| **Tier 2** | +10% Attack / Accuracy | Moderate Evasion boost |
| **Tier 3** | +20% All Combat Stats | Major Speed and Evasion |

<Callout type="info" title="Boss Respawn Timing">
The Holy Stone Keeper respawns three minutes after being defeated. Successful factions must maintain control of the center to prevent the enemy from stealing the next buff cycle.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Sacred Weapons Strategy in PvP">

Sacred Weapons are the ultimate battlefield tools in **rf online next chip war**. These are not standard equipment but limited-use assets that can break stalwarts or demolish defenses in seconds. Understanding when to pilot a MAU or deploy a Launcher is the difference between a failed raid and a successful chip break.

| Sacred Weapon | Battlefield Role | Best Usage Scenario |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **MAU** | Piloted Frontline Tank | Charging into enemy excavator rooms |
| **Launcher** | Heavy Fire Support | Obstructing enemy retreat routes |
| **Animus** | Summoned Controller | Forcing enemy repositioning during boss fights |

**Sacred Weapon Deployment Checklist:**
- **Timing**: Save deployments for when the Holy Stone Keeper is at 10% HP or when pushing an enemy barricade.
- **Positioning**: Launchers should be placed on high ground or behind friendly frontline units.
- **Coordination**: Never deploy a MAU solo; always ensure you have a **Technician** Biosuit nearby for repairs and support.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
The "Steely Watcher" achievement requires five Sacred Weapon deployments. Use the early stages of the war to practice deployment timing before the final high-stakes push.
</Callout>

</Section>
