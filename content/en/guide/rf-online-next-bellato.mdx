Sacred Weapons are not standard gear slots; they are massive, battlefield-scale tools designed to turn the tide of large-scale engagements. For the Bellato faction, the signature Sacred Weapon is the MAU (Mechanic Armor Unit). <Section icon={Target} title="Mining War & Faction PvP Strategies">

The Mining War (Faction War) is the ultimate competitive arena where thousands of players clash for resource dominance. In this mode, vertical space, base defense, and objective control are paramount. The conflict centers around base excavators, which must be protected at all costs while your faction attempts to dismantle the opposing bases.

A unique mechanic of the Mining War is that Excavator HP is reduced by the number of hits landed on it, rather than raw damage values. This means fast-attacking Biosuits and highly coordinated group strikes are much more effective than slow, heavy hits. Additionally, the Holy Stone Keeper spawns in the center map one minute after the war begins. Securing the final hit on this boss grants powerful, scaling combat buffs to your entire faction.

| Objective | Spawn Time | Mechanics | Faction Benefit |
| Excavator | War Start | HP drops by hit count | Protecting yours and destroying enemy base wins the war |
| Holy Stone Keeper | 1 Minute | Respawns 3 mins after defeat | Grants excavator barriers and scaling combat buffs |
| Airfield | 10 Minutes | Base-to-base connection | Allows high-altitude flanking, subject to fuel limits |
| Turrets & Barricades | Post-Keeper | Activated by Keeper control | Turrets destroy barricades, opening base attack paths |

<Callout type="success" title="Excavator Focus">
When attacking an enemy base, coordinate with your guild to use high-attack-speed Biosuits. Since excavator HP scales by hit count, speed always beats raw damage.
</Callout>
