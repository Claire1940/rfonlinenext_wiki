<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Dark Hole Walkthrough">

Navigating the Dark Hole requires structured execution. The dungeon is divided into distinct phases, each demanding a shift in tactical focus. Follow this step-by-step progression path to secure a clean dungeon clear.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Dungeon Entry & Buff Preparation">
    Before triggering the rift console, ensure all squad members have activated their food buffs and defense elixirs. Align your starting Biosuits to handle initial wave clearing. Having at least one player equipped with a defensive suit like the Enforcer is critical for grouping early spawns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clearing the Dimensional Waves">
    Once the rift opens, waves of enemies will assault the central pylon. Focus on utilizing high-AoE physical damage. The Demolisher Biosuit excels in this phase, allowing squads to clear packed groups of monsters quickly. Do not let the monsters overwhelm the pylon, as its durability determines your final clear rank.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activating the Sacred Pylons">
    Three mini-bosses will spawn at different corners of the chamber, shielding the central rift. The squad must split or rotate quickly to defeat these guardians. Each guardian possesses a specific shield type that can only be broken by matching Biosuit elements or high-impact physical attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeating the Void Keeper Boss">
    The final phase unleashes the Void Keeper. This boss features massive area-of-effect attacks, ground hazards, and a phase-transition shield. Coordinate your Sacred Weapon deployments here to break the shield, then swap to your highest single-target DPS Biosuits to burn down the boss's remaining health.
  </Step>
</Steps>

During the final boss fight, positioning is everything. The Void Keeper will periodically summon gravity wells that pull players toward high-damage zones. Using your Biosuit’s flight capabilities can help you escape these hazards and reposition to safe platforms.

<Callout type="warning" title="Hazard Alert">
If the central pylon's durability drops to zero during the wave phase, the dungeon will immediately fail, consuming your entry key without granting any rewards. Always prioritize defending the objective over chasing individual enemies.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Sacred Weapons Deployment & Boss Tactics">

Sacred Weapons are massive battlefield tools that can turn the tide of any fight. In the Dark Hole, deploying your MAU (Mechanic Armor Unit), Launcher, or Animus at the correct moment is the difference between a successful clear and a squad wipe. Because these weapons have long cooldowns and high resource costs, their usage must be coordinated across the entire squad.

During the final boss encounter, the Void Keeper will enter a "Stasis Shield" phase at 50% health. Normal attacks deal negligible damage to this shield. This is the optimal window to deploy your heavy machinery.

| Sacred Weapon | Best Deployment Moment | Battlefield Utility | Cooldown Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| **MAU (Mechanic Armor Unit)** | Boss Shield Phase | Frontline assault, massive shield-break damage | Rotate deployment with other squad tanks |
| **Launcher** | Wave Phase & Boss Stasis | Long-range artillery, clearing grouped targets | Deploy from elevated, safe platforms |
| **Animus** | Final Burn Phase (Under 20% HP) | High-burst damage, squad offensive buffs | Save for the final execute window |

When piloting a MAU, focus entirely on the boss's shield. The heavy physical impact of the MAU's melee attacks strips away boss barriers much faster than standard Biosuit skills. Meanwhile, players utilizing Launchers should maintain a safe distance, raining down fire support while avoiding ground hazards.

<Callout type="success" title="Coordinated Strike">
If all four squad members deploy their Sacred Weapons simultaneously during the shield phase, it triggers a "Steely Watcher" synergy, instantly stunning the boss for 8 seconds and doubling all incoming damage.
</Callout>
