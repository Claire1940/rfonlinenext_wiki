<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Biosuits for Efficient Field Farming">

Your farming speed is heavily determined by your active Biosuit. Unlike traditional MMORPGs, RF Online Next allows you to swap Biosuits on the fly to adapt to different combat scenarios. For field farming, where you face large clusters of monsters, area-of-effect (AoE) damage and ranged safety are the most valuable traits.

The Demolisher and Punisher stand out as S-Tier farming options due to their high damage output and range. The Demolisher specializes in devastating physical AoE attacks, making it perfect for clearing dense monster waves in open-world zones. The Punisher offers highly stable ranged damage and excellent cover capabilities, allowing you to farm safely from a distance.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Gear Enhancement and Stat Farming Priorities">

Farming resources is only half the battle; investing them wisely is what separates top-tier players from the rest. The game's progression system features multiple layers of enhancement, including Equipment Enhancement (Enhance, Batch Enhance, Prime Mod, and Random Options), Skill Power Growth, Collection Stats, and Rover Tuning.

When upgrading your gear, prioritize safe enhancement thresholds before attempting high-risk upgrades. If you find your farming speed slowing down or monsters hitting too hard, focus on your active equipment rather than gambling on new gear sets. 

| System | Priority | Key Farmed Stat | Optimal Progression Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Equipment Enhancement** | Core | Attack / Defense | Focus on active gear; use Prime Mod layers |
| **Skill Power Growth** | High | Skill Damage / Add-ons | Upgrade active skills only; avoid raw waste |
| **Collection Stats** | High | Accuracy / Attack | Complete collections to secure passive bonuses |
| **Rover Upgrades** | Medium | EXP Gain / Utility | Align Rover Tuning with your main combat role |

Your collection book is a massive source of permanent passive stats. When completing collections, always prioritize **Attack and Accuracy**. Attack directly increases your clearing speed, while Accuracy ensures your attacks land consistently on higher-level monsters, preventing wasted skill cooldowns.

Rovers act as passive companion units that float alongside your character. Systems like Rover Fuse, Collection, Tuning, and Rover Burst provide substantial stat bonuses. Focus on tuning your Rovers to grant bonuses to EXP gain, healing item capacity, and raw attack power to prolong your farming sessions.

<Callout type="warning" title="Upgrade Resource Management">
Avoid the temptation to upgrade every Biosuit and skill in your menu just because a red notification dot appears. Focus your resources strictly on your primary farming build to avoid running out of credits and upgrade kits.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mining War and Sacred Weapon Farming">

The legendary Mining War is the pinnacle of faction-based conflict in RF Online Next. This event is not just a PvP battleground; it is a vital farming mechanic that determines your faction's access to premium resources. The war revolves around controlling excavators, which lose HP based on the number of hits they take rather than raw damage numbers.

The **Holy Stone Keeper** is the most critical objective on the battlefield. It spawns in the center of the map one minute after the Faction War begins and respawns three minutes after being defeated. Landing the final hit on the Holy Stone Keeper grants your faction an escalating combat buff that increases movement speed, attack, defense, accuracy, and evasion. It also activates your faction's defensive turrets to destroy enemy barricades.

To dominate these massive engagements, players must master the deployment of Sacred Weapons. These are battlefield-scale tools that can completely turn the tide of a fight.

| Sacred Weapon | Type | Battlefield Role | Optimal Tactical Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **MAU (Mechanic Armor Unit)** | Piloted Machine | Frontline Assault | Direct pressure, breaking defensive lines |
| **Launcher** | Heavy Support Weapon | Ranged Firepower | Area denial, covering lanes and objectives |
| **Animus** | Summoned Power | Battlefield Control | Large-scale burst presence, turning team fights |

Deploying these weapons requires precise timing and coordination. For instance, launching a MAU assault to defend an excavator, or placing a Launcher to cut off enemy retreat routes near the airfield (which opens ten minutes into the match), can secure victory and unlock exclusive high-tier mining rights for your entire guild.

If your guild needs extra numbers, the Mercenary System allows guild leaders to recruit external players. Recruitment is open until 21:10, with prize rewards ranging from 100 to 100,000 Diamonds, offering a highly lucrative farming avenue for solo mercenary players.

<Callout type="tip" title="Sacred Weapon Deployment">
Complete your Sacred Weapon deployments whenever they are off cooldown. The Epic Games achievement **Steely Watcher** requires deploying Sacred Weapons five times, which serves as excellent practice for managing summon timings before high-stakes faction wars.
</Callout>
