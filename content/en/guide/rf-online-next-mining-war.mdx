<Section icon={Map} title="The Core Mechanics of RF Online Next Mining War">

The **rf online next mining war** represents the pinnacle of faction-based conflict in Netmarble’s sci-fi MMORPG. Unlike traditional PvP duels, this is a layered war system where thousands of players from the Bellato, Cora, and Accretia factions clash over industrial excavators. The primary objective is not just to kill enemies, but to systematically dismantle the opposing faction's infrastructure while protecting your own.

In the 2026 meta, success requires a deep understanding of the unique health mechanics applied to war objectives. Most notably, excavators do not possess standard HP bars that melt under high DPS. Instead, they operate on a hit-count system, making high-attack-speed Biosuits and coordinated multi-player strikes more effective than single high-damage bursts.

<Section icon={Target} title="The Holy Stone Keeper: Central Objective Strategy">

One minute after the **rf online next mining war** commences, the Holy Stone Keeper spawns at the center of the battlefield. This massive entity is the most critical secondary objective in the game. Defeating it provides the killing faction with a barrier for their excavators and a series of escalating combat buffs that can turn the tide of a losing battle.

The battle for the Keeper is often where the most intense PvP occurs. If the Keeper is defeated, it will respawn exactly three minutes later, creating a rhythmic cycle of "push and pull" throughout the war duration. Guild leaders must decide whether to commit their full force to the center or leave a detachment to harass enemy excavators.

| Buff Tier | Stat Bonuses | Duration / Condition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | Movement Speed +10%, Attack +5% | Active upon final hit |
| **Tier 2** | Defense +10%, Accuracy +15 | Scales with consecutive kills |
| **Tier 3** | Evasion +20, Critical Rate +5% | Maximum faction dominance |
| **Tier 4** | Excavator Barrier Activation | Protects base from minor incursions |

<Callout type="success" title="Tactical Priority">
Always prioritize the "Final Hit" on the Holy Stone Keeper. Even if your faction did 90% of the damage, the faction that lands the killing blow receives the full suite of buffs and activates their base turrets.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Aerial Tactics and Airfield Control">

Ten minutes into the **rf online next mining war**, the airfields for Bellato, Cora, and Accretia become active. This transition completely changes the geometry of the battlefield. Players can now bypass ground-level barricades and launch direct assaults on enemy excavators from the sky. However, flight is not a permanent state; it is a tactical resource that must be managed carefully.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor the 10-Minute Mark">
    Prepare your flight-capable Biosuits (like the Punisher or Demolisher) for immediate takeoff once the airfield opens.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fuel Level Check">
    Flying at high altitudes or engaging in prolonged aerial combat drains fuel rapidly. Running out of fuel mid-air results in a fatal fall.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Coordinate Flanking Maneuvers">
    Use the vertical space to bypass ground Guardians and turrets. Drop down directly onto the enemy excavator for a surprise hit-count burst.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Return to Base">
    Cycle players back to the ground to recharge fuel while a second wave takes to the skies, ensuring constant aerial pressure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Navigation Tip">
The airfield connects all three factions. This means that while you are flanking an enemy, your own base is equally vulnerable to an aerial counter-attack. Always leave "Enforcer" Biosuits on the ground for base defense.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Winning Conditions and Contribution Rewards">

Victory in the **rf online next mining war** is determined by a clear hierarchy of objectives. The most direct path to winning is the total destruction of all opposing faction excavators. However, in balanced matches, the winner is often decided by the remaining health (hit count) of the excavators at the end of the time limit.

| Outcome | Primary Condition | Reward Tier |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Victory** | All enemy excavators destroyed | Maximum Diamonds & Buffs |
| **Points Win** | Highest remaining excavator HP | High Contribution Rewards |
| **Draw** | Equal excavator HP remaining | Standard Participation Loot |
| **Defeat** | All friendly excavators destroyed | Minimum Consolation Rewards |

Individual contribution is tracked throughout the war. Staying near the boss, dealing objective damage, and participating in successful defensive stands will earn you "Adventure Coins" and progression materials. Note that changing maps during a boss fight or war objective can reset your contribution tally, so stay committed to your zone.
