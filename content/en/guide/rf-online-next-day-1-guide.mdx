<Section icon={Swords} title="Biosuits and Early Build Priorities">
RF Online Next features a flexible Biosuit system, allowing you to swap combat styles on the fly. For Day 1, selecting and focusing on an effective Biosuit is key for efficient solo play and questing.

**Biosuit Recommendations for Day 1:**

| Biosuit     | Role                     | Range           | Beginner Fit    | PvE Use                                 | PvP Use                                  |
|-------------|--------------------------|-----------------|-----------------|-----------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| **Demolisher** | Physical AoE Damage      | Mid to Long     | Very High       | Mob clearing, field farming, dungeon    | Area pressure, clustered damage          |
| **Punisher**   | Stable Ranged Damage     | Ranged          | High            | Safe questing, consistent attacks       | Cover fire, crowd control pressure       |
| **Enforcer**   | Frontline Guardian/Tank  | Close           | High            | Survival, multi-enemy pulls, boss learning | Shielding, taunt pressure, disruption    |
| **Dreadnought**| Melee Destroyer          | Close           | Medium-High     | Melee questing, direct burst            | Chasing targets, combo pressure          |

<Callout type="info" title="Biosuit Swapping">
Biosuits are your classes. You can swap them from the Biosuit menu. Focus on one damage-oriented suit first, then unlock others as tactical swap options for specific situations (e.g., a support Biosuit for party content).
</Callout>

Early progression should focus on enhancing your chosen Biosuit's gear and skills.

**Progression Priorities:**
1.  **Main Damage Biosuit**: Invest in the suit you use for most quests and dungeons.
2.  **Gear Enhancement**: Upgrade equipped gear through Enhance, Batch Enhance, Prime Mod.
3.  **Skill Power**: Prioritize skills you actively use. Don't upgrade unused skills just because they have a red dot.
4.  **Collection Stats**: Focus on Attack and Accuracy first for damage consistency.
5.  **Rovers**: Utilize Rovers for passive bonuses like EXP gain or attack stats.
