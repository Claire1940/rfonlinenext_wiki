<Section icon={Shield} title="Gear Enhancement and Stat Priorities">

As you progress past level 30, monster health and damage scale up significantly. If you find your character dying frequently or failing to clear quest objectives within the time limit, you are likely facing a stat bottleneck. To overcome this, you must actively upgrade your equipment, skills, and passive collections.

The progression system consists of several overlapping layers. Equipment enhancement includes basic level upgrades, batch enhancements, prime mods, and random option rolls. Skill growth involves adding new skills, leveling up active skills, using add-ons, and mastering finishing blows. Additionally, the collection system provides permanent passive stat boosts to your entire account.

| Stat Name | Leveling Priority | Primary Source | Impact on Progression |
|---|---|---|---|
| Attack | High | Weapon Enhancement / Prime Mods | Increases raw damage output to clear monsters faster |
| Accuracy | High | Collection Stats / Equipment Options | Prevents attacks from missing higher-level targets |
| Defense / HP | Medium | Armor Enhancement / Rover Bonuses | Increases survivability against elite quest bosses |
| EXP Gain | Medium | Rover Tuning / Collection Milestones | Accelerates the rate of level progression |

To allocate your resources efficiently, follow this progression priority checklist:

| Upgrade Layer | Core Resource Required | Target Level | Progression Benefit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Weapon Enhancement | Weapon Upgrade Stones | Level 1+ | Directly boosts your primary damage source |
| Skill Add-Ons | Skill Enhancement Books | Level 20+ | Modifies active skills for faster cooldowns or extra AoE |
| Collection Stats | Spare Equipment and Items | Level 30+ | Grants permanent account-wide passive stat bonuses |
| Rover Tuning | Rover Workshop Materials | Level 30+ | Unlocks companion bonuses like EXP gain and healing |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Avoid spreading your enhancement stones evenly across all your gear slots. Prioritize upgrading your primary weapon first, followed by gloves (for accuracy), and then your chest piece and boots for defense.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Faction War and Mining War Progression">

Large-scale PvP is not just an endgame activity; it is a vital component of your leveling process. Participating in the daily Mining War (Faction War) grants significant experience points, faction currency, and character buffs that accelerate your PvE farming speed.

The Mining War centers on controlling excavators located in the neutral zones. These excavators are highly durable, and their health is reduced by the total number of hits received rather than the raw damage value of each hit. This mechanic makes high-attack-speed Biosuits exceptionally valuable during excavation phases.

The battlefield features several key objectives that can turn the tide of the war:

- **The Holy Stone Keeper**: Spawns in the center of the map one minute after the war begins. Defeating this boss grants the capturing faction a powerful combat buff that scales across movement speed, attack, defense, accuracy, and evasion. It also activates defensive turrets to destroy enemy barricades.
- **The Airfield**: Opens ten minutes into the match, connecting the Accretia, Bellato, and Cora airfields. This allows for rapid aerial flanking maneuvers and vertical combat positioning.
- **The Mercenary System**: Players who are not part of a dominant guild can register as mercenaries before 21:10. Guild leaders can recruit these mercenaries using a prize pool ranging from 100 to 100,000 Diamonds, providing an excellent source of premium currency for solo levelers.
